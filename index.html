Development of the Internet

The internet was invented in 1969. Only in the 1990s, it was developed so far that the Internet becomes commercially available to the public. Internet usage has evolved all over the world with there around 4.66 billion users as of January 2021, close to 60% of the world`s total population. (datareportal.com, 2021)

In the developed world most people are using the Internet for communication, work, shopping, etc. purposes. Third-world countries are quickly catching up as technologies become cheaper to produce. In 2022 with Elon Musk`s Starlink technology the internet will be available in areas where it was not possible before. (Crist, 2021)
